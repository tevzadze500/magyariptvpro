FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

What devices are supported?

Magyar IPTV Pro works on Amazon Firestick, Android phones/boxes/TV, iOS (iPhone/iPad), Smart TVs (Samsung, LG), MAG boxes, and Windows/Mac PCs — using apps such as IPTV Smarters Pro or IBO Player.

How long does activation take?

Activation is fast. After you contact us on WhatsApp, we set up your access and hand it over ready to use within about 5-10 minutes.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes. We offer a full 24-hour, non-binding free trial so you can test the stability and quality risk-free. Just request it on WhatsApp.

What internet speed do I need?

We recommend a minimum of 15 Mbps for standard HD streaming, and at least 30 Mbps to enjoy our magyar iptv channels in true 4K without any buffering.

Can I use the subscription on multiple devices?

A standard subscription allows you to watch on one device at a time. However, you can install the playlist on as many devices as you want and simply switch between them.

How do I pay for the subscription?

We keep billing secure. Once you are 100% satisfied with your free trial, we will send a formal invoice via PayPal so you can pay safely.

I am not good with technology, can you help me set it up?

Absolutely! Customer support is our priority. Contact us on WhatsApp, and we will set up your magyar iptv service remotely in less than 5 minutes.
