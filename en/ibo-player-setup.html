The Setup

Activate IBO Player in 4 simple steps

No technical knowledge required — just follow along.

1

Install the App

Search for IBO Player on the Samsung or LG App Store, install it via the Firestick Downloader, or download it from the Android Play Store.

2

Note MAC Address & Key

Open the app on your TV. On the launch screen you will see two identifiers — the Device MAC address and the Device Key. Write both of them down.

3

Upload Playlist via Xtream Codes

Go to the official IBO Player upload portal, enter your MAC address and Key, then add the Portal URL, Username, and Password provided by Magyar IPTV Pro.

4

Restart & Stream

Reload the app and enjoy your 10,000+ premium 4K channels, movies, and series — all loaded automatically.

Troubleshooting

Common fixes & good to know

⚠️ Before you start

  • App activation (important): IBO Player includes a 7-day free trial. After that, the app itself requires a small one-time activation fee on its official website. This fee is for the app only and is completely independent of your Magyar IPTV Pro subscription.
  • Channels not loading? Fully close and restart the app to force your playlist to reload — new content usually appears within a few seconds.
  • Occasional freezing? Clear the app cache from your device settings for smoother, more stable playback.
