Troubleshooting

Common fixes & good to know

⚠️ Before you start

  • Free vs Premium (important): TiviMate works with a free version. Some advanced features (multiple playlists, recording, favorites sync) require the one-time TiviMate Premium unlock on Google Play. This is for the app only and is completely independent of your Magyar IPTV Pro subscription.
  • EPG not showing? Give it a minute to finish downloading the guide, or force a refresh in Settings → EPG.
  • Channels not loading? Reload the playlist in Settings → Playlists and restart the app — content usually appears within a few seconds.
