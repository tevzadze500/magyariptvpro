Installation Guide – Activate Magyar IPTV Pro in minutes

Follow the simple steps below to set up the service on any device.

Setup

Setting up IPTV Smarters Pro and Smart IPTV

1 Download – IPTV Smarters Pro or IBO Player

Download the recommended app — IPTV Smarters Pro or IBO Player. Both are free and available on every device:

  • ▸ Android / Android TV / Box: Google Play Store
  • ▸ iPhone / iPad: App Store
  • ▸ Amazon FireStick: Amazon Appstore or the Downloader app
  • ▸ Smart TV (Samsung, LG): your TV's app store — Smart IPTV or IPTV Smarters Pro
2 Log in

Open the app and choose "Login with Xtream Codes API". Enter the three details you received on WhatsApp:

Username received on WhatsApp
Password received on WhatsApp
Server URL received on WhatsApp

Save it — the content syncs within a few seconds.

3 Enjoy the show

You're all set! Enjoy the full Ultra 4K experience — more than 10,000 channels, the latest movies and series, buffer-free, on any device.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I receive my access details after activation?

Once your payment is confirmed, we send your access details (username, password, and server URL) via WhatsApp within 5 minutes at most. These details stay valid for the entire duration of your subscription.

Which app do you recommend?

We recommend IPTV Smarters Pro, IBO Player, and Smart IPTV. All are free and connect through the Xtream Codes API. They work reliably on Smart TVs, Android Boxes, FireSticks, mobile phones, and PCs.

Can I use the service on several devices at the same time?

Yes, depending on the plan you choose. The annual subscription includes 2 simultaneous connections (for example, the living-room TV and the bedroom phone). Smaller plans allow one active connection.

What should I do if the content does not load after setup?

In more than 90% of cases, the cause is an internet connection below 10 Mbps or a router that needs restarting. Check your speed at fast.com, restart your router, and reload the app. If the problem persists, message us on WhatsApp for direct help.
